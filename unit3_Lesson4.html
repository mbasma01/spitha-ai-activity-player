The Coach

Supervised Learning

In this mode, the AI is a student. We provide the Training Set (the questions) and the Labels (the answers).

  • ✔ Goal: Mapping inputs to known outputs.
  • ✔ Analogy: Learning a new language with a dictionary.
"If you know exactly what the answer should look like (e.g., 'Spam' or '$500'), use Supervised."
🔭

The Scientist

Unsupervised Learning

In this mode, the AI is an explorer. We provide raw data but no answers. The AI finds its own logic.

  • ✔ Goal: Discovering structure or hidden patterns.
  • ✔ Analogy: Exploring a dark cave with only a flashlight.
"If you aren't sure what categories exist in your data yet, use Unsupervised."
