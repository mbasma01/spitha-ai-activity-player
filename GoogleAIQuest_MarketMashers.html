Field Notes for Learners

You are about to enter a simulation where you control a digital kiosk. Before you start, remember:

  • The Goal: You need to predict sales to avoid wasting food (inventory).
  • Data is Messy: Look closely at the "3D" graphs. Can you spot the outliers?
  • Training Matters: When training your model, you aren't just drawing a line; you are minimizing the Error Distance (the space between the dots and the line).
  • Iterate: You might not get 100% accuracy on the first try. That is normal in Data Science! Adjust your parameters and try again.
