Strategy Guide & Walkthrough

Strategy: You will be presented with potential data sources (Temperature, Day of Week, etc.). You must select only the ones that show a strong Linear Trend.

Tip: If the dots on the scatter plot look like a random cloud, that feature is "Noise". If they form a line (up or down), select it!

NaN (Not a Number): These are missing values. The standard fix is to use Imputation (replacing the blank spot with the Average/Mean of the other days).

Outliers: Look for dots that are physically impossible (e.g., Temperature = 500°C). These are sensor glitches. You must remove them, or they will ruin your model's accuracy.

When you train the model, you are trying to make the red "Error Lines" (Residuals) as short as possible.

Pro Tip: Adjust the Learning Rate if your model is jumping around too much, and increase Epochs if it hasn't finished learning yet.
