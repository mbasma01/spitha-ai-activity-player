Lesson 12: Model Performance Part 2

Underfitting

When AI fails because it's too simple, too lazy, or simply doesn't have enough evidence to see the truth.

📉 The "Blind" Model

Underfitting happens when the model is "too rigid" to follow the data. It's the opposite of overfitting.

It's like a student who only reads the Summary of a book. They know the main theme, but they fail the test because they missed all the details.

"Underfitting models have High Bias. They have already 'made up their mind' and ignore specific evidence."

Diagnostic Check
