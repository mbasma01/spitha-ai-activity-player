What Makes Something "Smart"?

At its core, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is about creating systems that can perform tasks normally requiring human thinking or reasoning.

  • It involves learning from data, making judgments under uncertainty, and adapting to new information.
  • What is NOT AI? Anything that follows a simple, predefined, unchanging sequence of steps or a clear "if A, then B" rule.
