The Refresher: Choosing Your Tools

Before we build the automation machine, we need to verify our raw materials. Data Science is about choosing the right "Container" for your data. If you choose the wrong one, the machine will jam.

The List []

Ordered sequence. Best for items where position matters (1st, 2nd, 3rd). Like airplane seats.

The Dictionary {}

Key-Value pairs. Best for items with unique Labels. Like a library card catalog.

The DataFrame

Rows & Columns. Best for complex datasets with mixed types. Like an Excel sheet.
